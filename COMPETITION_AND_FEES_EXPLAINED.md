## 4️⃣ **What is "Referral Fee"?**

### **Referral Fee = Amazon's Commission**

Think of it like **rent** for selling on Amazon's platform.

### **What You Get for This Fee:**

✅ **Access to millions of customers**
- Amazon.eg visitors see your product
- Trust in Amazon brand
- Amazon search results

✅ **Payment processing**
- Amazon handles all payments
- No need for payment gateway
- Secure transactions

✅ **Customer service**
- Amazon handles returns
- A-to-Z guarantee
- Customer support

### **Is It Necessary?**

**YES - You CANNOT avoid it!**

Every seller on Amazon pays referral fees:
- It's automatically deducted from each sale
- No negotiation possible
- Same for all sellers in the category

**It's the cost of doing business on Amazon** 💼
